About Me

Personal Details

Near the end of my engineering undergraduate studies, I felt lost and unsure about what type of career I wanted. Ultimately I decided to continue studying and pursued a master's degree in engineering. Fortunately, early on in my post-graduate studies, I stumbled upon an introductory data science course being offered within my department. Ever since then, I've been motivated to learn more about machine learning and working with data via graduate courses, books, and online resources.

In my spare time, I love coding personal projects and learning new technologies. I am the type of person who constantly strives to improve and learn something new every day.

Outside of my career interests, I enjoy strength training. I also love playing video games, mainly competitive strategy games, RPGs, and rhythm games.
